How to Play

In Slope, you control a ball racing down a constantly changing track. Your goal is to survive as long as possible by dodging obstacles and staying on the path while the speed increases rapidly.

Controls

Use A/D or the Left/Right Arrow keys to steer.

How to Play

Tag is a fast-paced multiplayer chase game. One player is “it” and must catch the others before time runs out. Each round throws you into chaotic arenas where movement and strategy matter more than luck.

Controls

Use W/A/S/D or Arrow keys to move. If you’re “it,” your goal is to tag another player. If not, run, dodge, and survive.

Developer's Review

Tag is chaotic in the best way. It’s perfect for quick sessions with friends and always feels competitive. Clean mechanics, fun maps, and nonstop action. 4.7/5 stars.
